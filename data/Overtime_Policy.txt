Company Name: Acme Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Document Title: Overtime Policy
Effective Date: June 1, 2022

1. Eligibility
- Non-managerial employees are eligible for overtime pay.

2. Rates
- Overtime will be paid at 1.5x the hourly rate for hours exceeding 9 per day or 48 per week.

3. Approval
- Prior approval from the reporting manager is mandatory for overtime work.
