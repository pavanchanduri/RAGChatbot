Company Name: Acme Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Document Title: Equal Opportunity Policy
Effective Date: March 1, 2022

1. Statement of Policy
Acme Technologies is committed to providing equal opportunities in employment without discrimination based on gender, age, race, religion, disability, or sexual orientation.

2. Recruitment
- All job postings and interviews will follow non-discriminatory practices.
- Hiring decisions will be based solely on qualifications and merit.

3. Training
- HR will conduct awareness sessions on inclusivity and diversity annually.

4. Reporting
- Employees may report discrimination incidents to HR confidentially.
