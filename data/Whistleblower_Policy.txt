Company Name: Acme Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Document Title: Whistleblower Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2023

1. Purpose
To encourage employees to report unethical practices or policy violations.

2. Protection
- Whistleblowers will be protected against retaliation.
- Anonymous reporting is allowed through the HR portal.

3. Reporting Channels
- Employees may report concerns to HR, compliance, or the designated whistleblower officer.

4. Investigation
- All cases will be investigated fairly and promptly.
