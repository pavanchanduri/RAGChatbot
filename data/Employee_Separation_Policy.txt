Company Name: Acme Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Document Title: Employee Separation Policy
Effective Date: October 1, 2022

1. Resignation
- Employees must provide a minimum of 30 days written notice.

2. Termination
- The company reserves the right to terminate employment due to misconduct, redundancy, or performance issues.

3. Exit Interview
- All separating employees must complete an exit interview with HR.

4. Final Settlement
- Full and final settlement will be processed within 45 days of separation.
