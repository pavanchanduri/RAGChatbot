Company Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave: Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
2. Sick Leave: Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for absences longer than 2 days.
3. Maternity Leave: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of maternity leave as per government regulations.
4. Paternity Leave: Male employees are entitled to 10 days of paternity leave.
5. Unpaid Leave: Employees may request unpaid leave subject to managerial approval.
