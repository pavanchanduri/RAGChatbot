Code of Conduct

1. Professionalism: Employees must maintain a high standard of professionalism in all interactions.
2. Integrity: Employees are expected to act with honesty and integrity at all times.
3. Harassment: The company has zero tolerance for harassment, bullying, or discrimination.
4. Confidentiality: Sensitive company information must not be shared with unauthorized parties.
5. Compliance: Employees must comply with all company policies and legal regulations.
