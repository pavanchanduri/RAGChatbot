Company Name: Acme Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Document Title: Business Ethics Policy
Effective Date: April 1, 2022

1. Integrity
Employees are expected to conduct themselves with integrity in all business dealings.

2. Conflict of Interest
- Employees must disclose any personal or financial interests that conflict with company interests.

3. Bribery
- Offering or accepting bribes is strictly prohibited.

4. Compliance
- Violations will result in disciplinary action up to termination.
