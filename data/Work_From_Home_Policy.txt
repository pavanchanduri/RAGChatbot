Work From Home Policy

1. Eligibility: Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week with prior manager approval.
2. Availability: Employees must be available during core business hours (9 AM - 5 PM).
3. Equipment: The company will provide necessary equipment such as laptops and headsets.
4. Data Security: Employees must ensure secure internet connections and follow IT security protocols.
5. Performance: Remote work performance will be monitored and reviewed quarterly.
