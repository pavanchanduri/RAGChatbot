Company Name: Acme Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Document Title: Anti-Harassment Policy
Effective Date: August 1, 2022

1. Policy Statement
Harassment of any form—verbal, physical, or visual—is strictly prohibited.

2. Reporting
- Employees should report incidents directly to HR or via the anonymous reporting system.
- All complaints will be investigated within 10 working days.

3. Disciplinary Action
- Violators may face warnings, suspension, or termination.

4. Awareness
- Annual training on harassment prevention will be mandatory for all employees.
