Company Name: Acme Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Document Title: Training and Development Policy
Effective Date: February 1, 2023

1. Objective
To enhance employee skills and ensure career growth through continuous learning.

2. Training Programs
- Induction training for new hires.
- Technical and leadership training workshops.

3. Funding
- The company will reimburse fees for job-related certifications approved by managers.

4. Evaluation
- Training effectiveness will be measured through post-training assessments.
