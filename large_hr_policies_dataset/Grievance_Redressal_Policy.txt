Company Name: Acme Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Document Title: Grievance Redressal Policy
Effective Date: July 1, 2022

1. Purpose
To provide a formal process for employees to raise workplace concerns and complaints.

2. Process
- Complaints must be submitted in writing to the HR department.
- HR will acknowledge the complaint within 3 business days.

3. Resolution
- Investigations will be completed within 15 business days.
- Findings and resolutions will be documented and shared with the complainant.

4. Confidentiality
- All grievances will be handled with strict confidentiality.
