Company Name: Acme Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Document Title: Work From Home Allowance Policy
Effective Date: September 1, 2021

1. Purpose
To support employees with additional expenses incurred while working remotely.

2. Allowances
- A monthly allowance of INR 1,500 will be provided for internet and electricity costs.
- Employees can claim reimbursement for ergonomic chairs and desks up to INR 10,000.

3. Conditions
- Allowance is applicable only for approved remote workers.
